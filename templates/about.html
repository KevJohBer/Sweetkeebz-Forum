Sweetkeebz Forum

About

Sweetkeebz Forum is an online forum for users who share the hobby of building mechanical keyboards. On this website users can share news about the mechanical keyboards scene, showcase their own projects, products or services or ask questions and recive information that will benefit the user.

History

Sweetkeebz started out as an idea about a company in Europe that sells mechanical keyboard related products such as DIY kits, keycaps, switches or even build services. Since then it has grown and taken on many forms in the mechanical keyboard scene, arranging events, building typetests and hosting competitions. Now Sweetkeebz has created a forum which is home to anyone who has content or questions about mechanical keyboards to share.

Goals

The goal with this website is to create a go-to place for people who share the mechanical keyboard hobby. The hobby is vast, and there are hundreds of forums and shops which you can explore. The goal of sweetkeebz is to keep everything in one place while still giving credit to the companies creating the products. We want users to feel less intimidated by the scene and to more easily find the products and information that they are looking for so that anyone can have that premium feel when working.
